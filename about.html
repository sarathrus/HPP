About Us

Our Story

Hocus Pocus Pictures was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate filmmakers with a shared vision: to create compelling short films that captivate audiences and tell meaningful stories.

Based in the Gold Coast, Australia, our team brings together diverse talents and perspectives to create unique cinematic experiences. We believe in the power of storytelling to connect people, inspire change, and spark imagination.

Our name, "Hocus Pocus," reflects our dedication to bringing magic to the screen through innovative filmmaking techniques and authentic narratives.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality short films that engage audiences emotionally and intellectually, while providing a platform for emerging talent in the Australian film industry.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading independent film production company that consistently delivers innovative and thought-provoking content, contributing to the rich tapestry of Australian cinema.
